Announcements

Special event in honor of Serge Abiteboul

Serge Abiteboul is a researcher and a pioneer in the fields of data management, database theory, and finite model theory. In 2023-2024, he is celebrating his 70th birthday and his retirement from Arcep, France's telecommunication regulation agency, after a long career as a computer scientist, conducted in large part at Inria. Serge was elected a member of the French Academy of Sciences in 2008, of the Academia Europaea in 2011, and an ACM Fellow in 2011.

To celebrate his scientific career and achievements, a scientific and festive event will be organized in his honor on the occasion of ICDT 2024 in Paestum, Italy, the 27th edition of a conference that Serge helped create in 1986, first held in Rome.

The event will be held on the afternoon of March 24, both in Paestum and online.

A detailed program and further updates will be posted in the coming weeks at the event's dedicated page.

In-person/online attendance

EDBT/ICDT was the first database conference to shift to an online format due to the pandemic outbreak in 2020.

Despite our great experience with online conferences, we learned that in-person experience remains the most valuable especially for young researchers and newcomers.

Therefore, the 2024 conference will be organized primarily as an in-person event and the organizers strongly encourage all participants to attend in person so they can get the most out of the conference.

However, the conference will have an online component, which allows participants to both attend the conference online and to give online presentations, in case of travel restrictions or other exceptional situations.


EDBT: 27th International Conference on Extending Database Technology, 25th March - 28th March, 2024

The EDBT series of conferences is an established and prestigious forum for the exchange of the latest research results in data management. Held in an attractive European location, the conference provides unique opportunities for database researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to explore new ideas, techniques, and tools, and to exchange experiences. The previous EDBT events were held in Venice, Vienna, Cambridge, Avignon, Valencia, Konstanz, Prague, Heraklion, Munich, Nantes, St Petersburg, Lausanne, Uppsala, Berlin, Genoa, Athens, Brussels, Bordeaux, Venice, Vienna, Lisbon, Copenhagen (virtual), Nicosia (virtual), Edinburgh (virtual), and Ioannina.

ICDT: 27th International Conference on Database Theory, 25th March - 28th March, 2024

ICDT is an established and prestigious series of international conferences on research in the foundations and theory of data management. The previous ICDT events were held in Rome, Bruges, Paris, Berlin, Prague, Delphi, Jerusalem, London, Siena, Edinburgh, Barcelona, St Petersburg, Lausanne, Uppsala, Berlin, Genoa, Athens, Brussels, Bordeaux, Venice, Vienna, Lisbon, Copenhagen (virtual), Nicosia (virtual), Edinburgh (virtual), and Ioannina. Since 2009, ICDT is held annually and jointly with EDBT, following the successful SIGMOD/PODS model.
