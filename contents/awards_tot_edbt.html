EDBT 2024 Test of Time Award

Established in 2014, the Test-of-Time Award awarded by the Extended Database Technology (EDBT) Conference recognizes papers presented at EDBT Conferences that have had the most impact in terms of research, methodology, conceptual contribution, or transfer to practice over the past ten years.

The EDBT 2024 Test of Time Award committee was formed by:

  • Minos Garofalakis (Chair)
  • Davide Martinenghi
  • Baihua Zheng

After careful consideration, the committee has decided to select the following paper as the EDBT ToT Award winner for 2024:

Reachability Queries in Very Large Graphs: A Fast Refined Online Search Approach
authored by
Renê R. Veloso, Loïc Cerf, Wagner Meira Jr, and Mohammed J. Zaki

Honorable Mention:

Exploiting the query structure for efficient join ordering in SPARQL queries
authored by
Andrey Gubichev and Thomas Neumann
