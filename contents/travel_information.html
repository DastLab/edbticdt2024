Getting to Paestum


via Naples:

The best way to reach Paestum is by plane via Naples. The Naples International Airport (NAP) has direct flights to and from most european hub airports
It is located approximately 5.9 kilometers north-east of the city of Naples in the Capodichino district.
The airport is effectively organized and connected to the Naples Railway Station through public transportation (Alibus), and to Salerno city with several buses (Flixbus, FS Bus Italia, Sita trasporti). Alibus runs with a frequency of 2-3 rides per hour, and the trip lasts about 20 minutes. Tickets can be purchases on board or through the App Unicocampania, and cost 5€.
Air buses to Salerno leave from a bus parking distant about 300 meters from the airstation, along the direction towards car rentals. They leave at 9:15 and 11:30am from Monday to Saturday and the trip lasts about 75 minutes. Tickets can be purchased either on board at about 7€, within stores in the airstation selling Unico Tickets (you need to buy Tic6 ticket at 6,40€), or through the App Unicocampania (Ticket Aircampania from Naples to Salerno at 5,50€).
BusItalia buses to Salerno leave from the same parking at 11:35am and 5:30pm from Monday to Saturday. Trip duration and tickets similar to Air bus. Similar modalities for Sita buses to Salerno, which leave at 8:45am and 4pm from Monday to Friday.
If you are travelling on Sunday: the trips from Naples are operated by Cosat, which leaves from the airport entrance at 8:30am, 12:05pm and 5:30pm on Sundays only.
For all of them get off in Salerno at Piazza Concordia stop, which is quite close to Salerno Railway Station.
Cosat also provides a terminal stop at the Battipaglia railway station.

Once reached either Naples, Salerno or Battipaglia railway stations you can get a Regional Train to either Capaccio-Roccadaspide or Paestum (Please consult Trenitalia for timetables) whose trip lasts about 70 minutes from Naples, 35 minutes from Salerno, or 10 minutes for Battipaglia.
Notice that Paestum's hotels can provide shuttles to reach your accomodation from both the Capaccio-Roccaspide and Paestum stations.

Examples of routes to Paestum:
  • From Naples Capodichino Airport (NAP) -> Airport Shuttle to Naples Central Station (Alibus airport shuttle) - 20 minutes -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 70 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 2 hours.
  • From Naples Capodichino Airport (NAP) -> Bus to Salerno (Flixbus, FS Bus Italia, Sita trasporti) - 75 minutes -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 35 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 2 hours.
  • (Sundays only) From Naples Capodichino Airport (NAP) -> Bus to Salerno (Cosat) - 50 minutes -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 35 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 1 hour and 25 minutes.
  • (Sundays only) From Naples Capodichino Airport (NAP) -> Bus to Battipaglia (Cosat) - 90 minutes -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 10 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 1 hour and 40 minutes.

via Rome:

Another way to reach Paestum is by plane via Rome, which is served by both the Fiumicino (FCO) and Ciampino (CIA) airports.
Both airports are effectively organized and connected to the Roma Termini Station by train (Trenitalia) and/or by Shuttles.
From the International Airport of Fiumicino there is the Leonardo Train to the Roma Termini Station, whose trip lasts 32 minutes, whereas from Ciampino Airport there is the Airlink Train also to the Roma Termini Station, whose trip lasts 15 minutes. Timetables can be consulted on the Trenitalia website.

Once reached the Roma Termini station, participants can get a high-speed train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo), taking about 2 hours, and then a Regional Train from Salerno to either Capaccio-Roccadaspide or Paestum (Please consult the Trenitalia's website) taking abount 35 minutes.
Notice that Paestum's hotels can provide shuttles to reach your accomodation from both the Capaccio-Roccaspide and Paestum stations.

Another alternative from Roma Termini could be to get a high-speed train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Trenitalia or Italo), taking about 2 hours and 50 minutes. Agropoli-Castellabate station is about 12 kilometers from Paestum, and there is a Taxi station to connect to your hotel.

Examples of routes to Paestum:
  • From Fiumicino Airport (FCO) -> Leonardo Express Train to Roma Termini Station (Leonardo Express) - 32 minutes -> High-speed Train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) - 2 hours -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 35 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 4 hours.
  • From Fiumicino Airport (FCO) -> Leonardo Express Train to Roma Termini Station (Leonardo Express) - 32 minutes -> High-speed Train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Trenitalia or Italo) - 2 hours and 50 minutes -> Taxi - 30 minutes. The whole trip lasts about 4,5 hours.
  • From Ciampino Airport (CIA) -> Ciampino Airlink to Roma Termini Station (Ciampino Airlink) - 15 minutes -> High speed Train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) - 2 hours -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) - 35 minutes -> Hotel Shuttle - few minutes. The whole trip lasts about 4 hours.
  • From Ciampino Airport (CIA) -> Ciampino Airlink to Roma Termini Station (Ciampino Airlink) - 15 minutes -> High speed Train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Ciampino Airlink) - 2 hours and 50 minutes -> Taxi - 30 minutes. The whole trip lasts about 4,5 hours.
