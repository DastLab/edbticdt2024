Travel Information


Getting to Paestum


via Naples:

The best way to reach Paestum is by air via Naples. The Naples International Airport (NAP) is well connected with the rest of the world.
It is located approximately 5.9 kilometers north-east of the city of Naples in the Capodichino district.
The airport is effectively organized and connected to the Naples Central Station with public transportation (Alibus), and to Salerno city with several buses (Flixbus, FS Bus Italia, Sita trasporti).

Once reached either Naples or Salerno central stations you can get a Regional Train to either Capaccio-Roccadaspide or Paestum (Please consult https://www.trenitalia.com/en.html).
Notice that Paestum's hotels can provide shuttles to reach your accomodation from both the Capaccio-Roccaspide and Paestum stations.

Examples of routes to Paestum:
  • From Naples Capodichino Airport -> Bus to Salerno -> Regionale Train to Capaccio Station -> Hotel Shuttle
  • From Naples Capodichino Airport -> Airport Shuttle to Naples Central Station -> Regionale Train to Capaccio Station -> Hotel Shuttle

via Rome:

Another way to reach Paestum is by air via Rome, which is served by both the Fiumicino (FCO) and Ciampino (CIA) airports.
Both airports are effectively organized and connected to the Roma Termini Station by train (Trenitalia) and/or by Shuttles.

Once reached the Roma Termini station participants can get a high-speed train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) and then a Regional Train from Salerno to either Capaccio-Roccadaspide or Paestum (Please consult Trenitalia).
Notice that Paestum's hotels can provide shuttles to reach your accomodation from both the Capaccio-Roccaspide and Paestum stations.

Another alternative from Roma Termini could be to get a high-speed train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Trenitalia or Italo), a city very close to Paestum, and then book a Taxi from Agropoli-Castellabate to your hotel.

Examples of routes to Paestum:
  • From Fiumicino Aeroporto -> Leonardo Express Train to Roma Termini Station (Leonardo Express) -> High-speed Train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) -> Hotel Shuttle
  • From Fiumicino Aeroporto -> Leonardo Express Train to Roma Termini Station (Leonardo Express) -> High-speed Train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Trenitalia or Italo) -> Taxi
  • From Ciampino Aeroporto -> Ciampino Airlink to Roma Termini Station (Ciampino Airlink) -> High speed Train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) -> Regional Train to Paestum or Capaccio-Roccadaspide Station (Trenitalia) -> Hotel Shuttle
  • From Ciampino Aeroporto -> Ciampino Airlink to Roma Termini Station (Ciampino Airlink) -> High speed Train to Agropoli-Castellabate (Ciampino Airlink) -> High speed Train to Salerno (Trenitalia or Italo) -> Taxi
