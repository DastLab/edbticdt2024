EDBT Camera-ready Instructions

Congratulations on your accepted paper at EDBT 2024! We have collected some instructions that will help you prepare the camera-ready version of your paper. Please read this information carefully and refer to the main EDBT website and communications for important dates.

Submission process

Please upload your camera-ready file as a new version replacing the original submission. Please note that by submitting the camera-ready version, you accept that the paper is published under the Creative Commons license CC-by-nc-nd 4.0 by OpenProceedings.org.

Formatting guidelines for Third Round papers
  • Papers must be submitted in PDF format and according to the official EDBT ACM proceedings format specified here. Please make sure you are using the modified ACM LaTeX2e style files with ISBN and copyright information.
  • The template and a basic package can be downloaded here: sample-edbt2024.zip
  • The font size, margins, inter-column spacing, and line spacing in the templates must be kept unchanged (page size A4). Note that we deliberately removed items, such as keywords and CCS format, which should therefore not be contained in the camera-ready version of your paper.
  • The ISBN in the main latex file would be 978-3-89318-095-0. Please make sure the copyright notice is exactly as follows: "â’¸ 2024 Copyright held by the owner/author(s).   • Note that here is no copyright form to sign, since the copyright remains with the authors.

EDBT Regular research papers (as well as E&A papers) should be at most 12 pages (with additional unlimited pages for references), Visionary and short papers are at most 6 pages (with additional unlimited pages for references).

For Experiments & Analysis and Vision papers, please remove the prefix ("[Experiments & Analysis]", "[Vision Paper]") from the title.

Generate a single PDF file named paper-#ID.pdf where #ID is the paper id of your submission.

Please make sure that:

  • all fonts used in your paper are embedded in the submitted PDF file
  • PDF file size <= 5MB
  • your paper does not contain page numbers, keywords and CCS
  • your paper uses A4 paper format (!)
  • your paper conforms to all the formatting requirements (correct margins, correct footer on first page)
