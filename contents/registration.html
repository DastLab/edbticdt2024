Cancellation Policy

Please consider the following deadlines for cancelling your registration, provided that you are not the presenter of a conference/workshop paper included in the proceedings:

  • Cancellation received before February 10th, 2024: 100% refund
  • Cancellation received on or after February 10th, 2024 up to March, 13th 2024: 50% refund.
  • Cancellation received on and after March 14th, 2024: no refund.
All refunds will be processed after the end of the conference.
If the publication is included in the proceedings, there is no refund (regardless of the date).
