Keynote: Post-PhD Career: Things I Wish I Had Known

by Paolo Papotti

EURECOM, France

Abstract: A PhD in data management can lead to a number of different careers. There are examples of DB researches thriving in research labs, professors with amazing groups, and even CTOs leading start ups including one of the biggest social networks. Still, identifying the 'ideal' career path during postgraduate studies is a formidable challenge, also due to numerous influential factors, such as geographical location and the nature (public or private) of the institutions in question. In this talk, drawing upon my diverse experiences across three continents and in various roles, I will share some stories about my journey to shed some light on these dynamics. While there is no universal blueprint for decision-making, this presentation aims to equip participants with an understanding of the career landscape and what it entails when transitioning into their first post-PhD role.
