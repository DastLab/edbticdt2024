The ICDT 2024 Best Paper and Newcomer Award


After careful consideration, the ICDT program committee has decided to select the following contribution as the ICDT Best Paper winner for 2024:

Xiao Hu and Stavros Sintos, for:
Finding Smallest Witnesses for Conjunctive Queries

And the following contribution as the ICDT Best Newcomer winner for 2024:

Florent Capelli and Oliver Irwin, for:
Direct Access for Conjunctive Queries with Negation

Both papers will be presented in ICDT Session 1, 25 March 2024, 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM. The awards will be presented during the awards session on Wednesday, March 27, 2024, 2:30 PM - 4:00PM.
